Устраним левую рекурсию:

Expr -> Term E
E -> + Term E
E -> - Term E
E -> e

Term -> Mul T

T -> * Mul T
T -> e

Mul -> -Mul
Mul -> n
Mul -> (Expr)

Not terminal    | FIRST     | FOLLOW
Expr  (X)       | ( - + n   | $ )
E               | e + -     | $ )
Term  (R)       | ( - + n   | $ ) + -
T               |   e *     | $ ) + -
Mul   (M)       | ( - + n   | $ ) + - *
